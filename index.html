Explore Engineering Career Paths

Understand what different engineering roles actually do — the tools they use, projects they deliver, and how you can get there.

💧

Hydraulic Engineer

Design water infrastructure, dams, and hydropower systems

Tools: HEC-RAS, MATLAB, AutoCAD
Projects: Pumped storage plants, irrigation channels, flood modelling
🏗️

Structural Engineer

Analyse and design buildings, bridges, and foundations

Tools: ETABS, SAP2000, AutoCAD
Projects: Bridge design, high-rise structures, seismic analysis
⚡

Power Systems Engineer

Plan and optimise electrical grids and renewable energy systems

Tools: MATLAB, PSS/E, ETAP
Projects: Solar-wind hybrid grids, power plant protection, smart grids
⚙️

Embedded Systems Engineer

Develop firmware and real-time control systems for hardware

Tools: C/C++, Python, Simulink
Projects: IoT devices, industrial robots, automation controllers
🌱

Environmental Engineer

Assess environmental impacts and design sustainable solutions

Tools: GIS, Remote Sensing, Python
Projects: Carbon footprint studies, satellite land-use analysis
🪨

Geotechnical Engineer

Study soil and rock mechanics for foundations and tunnels

Tools: PLAXIS, SLOPE/W, AutoCAD
Projects: Metro tunnelling, slope stability, foundation design

About the Platform

Theory to Reality

T2R is an initiative designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering practice — helping students understand how theory becomes impact.

What is T2R?

T2R (Theory to Reality) is a structured learning platform built by working engineers and researchers who have walked the same path — from classrooms to real engineering projects across the world.

We host expert-led sessions, curated resources, and mentorship opportunities that help engineering students go beyond textbooks and gain the knowledge they need for industry-ready careers.
